How it works

Set up once. Steal the show every game.

01
Build Your Roster

Add each player's name, jersey number, and position. Takes a few minutes — and you only do it once.

02
Pick Songs. Generate Intros.

Search Apple Music for each player's walk-up song and trim where it starts. Then tap to generate their personalized AI announcement.

03
Tap. Watch the Moment Happen.

On game day, tap their card. Song drops. Stadium call fires. Song roars back full — then fades. About 15 seconds, total. Just like the big leagues. Hit DUGOUT for the National Anthem, air horns, and Quick Play playlists between innings.

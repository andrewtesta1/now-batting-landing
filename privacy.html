Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Now Batting ("we," "our," or "us") is built by Andrew Testa. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Now Batting iOS application and website at nowbattingapp.com.

Information We Collect

In the app: Now Batting stores all roster data — player names, jersey numbers, batting order, walk-up song selections, and AI-generated announcements — locally on your device using iOS storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the roster sharing feature.

Player photos: Optional. If you add a photo to a player's card, it is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to any server.

Roster sharing: If you use the Share Roster feature, your roster (names, numbers, song metadata, and AI announcements) is temporarily uploaded to our servers to generate a share link. Shared roster data is stored for a limited time and then deleted.

Email address: If you sign up on our website (nowbattingapp.com), your email address is collected and stored via Google Sheets to send you a TestFlight access link. We do not sell or share your email address.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI stadium announcements via our TTS service (ElevenLabs)
  • To enable roster sharing between coaches and parents
  • To send TestFlight access links to interested coaches (website signups only)
  • To monitor app stability and crashes (Sentry, anonymized)

Third-Party Services

Now Batting uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Music — Used for walk-up song search and playback. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • ElevenLabs — Used to generate AI-voiced player announcements. Player names and numbers are sent to ElevenLabs to synthesize audio. ElevenLabs does not retain this data for training purposes.
  • Vercel — Hosts our API and website. Processes API requests including TTS generation and roster sharing.
  • Sentry — Anonymized crash reporting. No personally identifiable information is included in crash reports.

Data Retention

Roster data stored locally on your device is retained until you delete the app or the data manually. Shared roster data on our servers is automatically deleted after 30 days. Email addresses collected via the website signup are retained until you request removal.

Children's Privacy

Now Batting is designed for use by coaches and parents, not children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Player names entered into the app are used solely to generate game-day announcements and are not shared with third parties beyond what is described above.

Your Rights

You can delete all locally stored app data by uninstalling Now Batting. To request removal of your email from our waitlist or deletion of any shared roster data, email us at drewtesta@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of Now Batting after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact
